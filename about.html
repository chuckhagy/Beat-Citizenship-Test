About Us

What is BCT?

Welcome to BeatCitizenshipTest.com. This website is an open source project designed to help US permanant residents prepare for (and pass!) their America citizenship test and interview. Right now, it focuses on the NC-400 interview and History/Civics portions of the test.

Our Story

This project was inspired by the students in the Centro Latino de San Francisco and made possible by the hard work and experience of their dedicated instructor, María Eugenia Sarti. If you are in San Fracisco, CA, we highly recommend joining their wonderful citizenship preparation classes. Otherwise, check out these other locations in other cities accross the US.

Get Involved

This site will hopefully continue to grow and improve as a resource, and our mission is to keep it free and available for anyone who wants to use it. If you'd like to get involved on the tech side, you can check out the GitHub repo here. Not tech-savvy? You can still help by sharing this site with someone that wants to earn their US citizenship. Or better yet, volunteer with a local organization near you!
